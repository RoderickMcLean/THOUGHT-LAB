About the Blog

An odd title for a blog, but future and ology are defined as such:

"Future - A period of time following the moment of speaking or writing; time regarded as still to come."

— Oxford Dictionary

"Ology - A subject of study; a branch of knowledge."

— Oxford Dictionary

So future-ology means the study of the future. Astro Teller, the head of the Google X Lab, puts eloquently the frame of mind that this blog aims to achieve:

Therefore, this blog was created to have and make up moonshot ideas of what is possible. It aims to inform, but more importantly to discuss the most recent technological breakthroughs and how we can we use them to change the world we live in.

We will also make predictions as well as far off guesses of what is to come. No idea is too crazy.

About the Author


Hi my name is Rod, and I'm slightly obsessed with trends and patterns. Not of behaviour or markets, but more in the sense of what we are tending towards technologically.


The world is now composed of extremely ambitious entrepenuers and thinkers that are trying to push what is achievable for the human race, to the very boundaries of our comprehension. These thinkers, and their wild thoughts, are what inspired me to begin this blog, and personal project, of creative thinking and investigation into the distant future of possibilities.


Currently working as a software tester in London, I get to sit on projects testing technologies on a daily basis. So now more than ever in my life, I have to beg the question of "what is to come?"


- Roderick McLean, author Of FutureOlogy
